panchanga API

Drik Panchanga (Smārta, pūrṇimānta) — tithi, nakṣatra, festivals & eclipses, as JSON.

Endpoints

All are GET, return JSON (except the .ics feed), and accept a location as either a place slug or explicit lat, lng & tz.

  • /api/panchanga?date=YYYY-MM-DD&place=calgary-ab — the day's pañcāṅga
  • /api/festivals?year=2026&place=calgary-ab — the year's festivals
  • /api/eclipses?year=2026&place=calgary-ab — grahaṇas (eclipses)
  • /api/calendar.ics?place=calgary-ab — subscribable iCalendar feed (add it to Google/Apple Calendar)
  • /api/places?q=austin — search the supported cities
  • /api — machine-readable usage & example places

Places

Every US & Canada city of 10,000+ people and India city/town of 15,000+ people (~7,200 total) is a slug like austin-tx, jersey-city-nj or jaipur-rj. A bare name picks the largest match (vancouver → Vancouver, BC); new-delhi, mumbai & london are kept too. Don't know the slug? Search /api/places?q=springfield.

Examples

curl "https://panchanga-five.vercel.app/api/festivals?year=2026&place=calgary-ab"
curl "https://panchanga-five.vercel.app/api/places?q=springfield&country=US"
curl "https://panchanga-five.vercel.app/api/panchanga?date=2026-11-08&lat=51.04&lng=-114.07&tz=America/Edmonton"
